About Us

Since its founding in 2013, Ithemba Home has been responsible for providing over R3.8 billion in assistance over 10 years. We offer help without conditions, regardless of race, religion, background, or social class. We work with communities, corporates, and volunteers to serve humanity for the greater good.

Overview

We work closely with communities, volunteers, and donors to uplift vulnerable women and children. Our approach focuses on dignity, sustainability, and long-term impact.

Our Purpose

Ithemba Home provides a safe haven for victims of abuse and hardship. We help women and children heal emotionally, rebuild their lives, and reintegrate into society as empowered individuals.

Our Impact

Through bursary programs, housing initiatives, and trauma-informed support, we’ve helped families find safety, education, and stability. Our volunteers are trained to respond compassionately to victims of abuse.
