Our Purpose

Ithemba Home aims to bridge that gap by providing a haven to the victims as well as help them improve their lives, heal their traumas, and help integrate them back to society to become upstanding citizens. We will build a supportive environment for women and children who have experienced abuse, violence, or in desperate need temporary housing and assistance.

Our Impact

We have built a bursary programme, providing opportunities to children to finish their education and woman who wish to pursue their tertiary education. Our programs have also helped volunteers to be more equipped to handle abuse or situations where there are victims. With the help of our donors, we have been able to provide women and children with proper housing and a safe environment that is healthy for children as well.
